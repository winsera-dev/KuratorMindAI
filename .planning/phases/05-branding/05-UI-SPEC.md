## 🏗️ Brand Pillar: "Forensic Minimalism"
The brand identity for KuratorMind is defined by **Absolute Precision** and **Expert Transparency**. It feels like a high-end forensic laboratory where complex insolvency data is synthesized into clear, legal insights.

### Core Visual Meta
- **Connectivity**: A minimalist "K" formed by neural nodes, representing agentic intelligence.
- **Forensic Tagging**: The "AI" is presented as a monospace evidence tag (JetBrains Mono style).
- **Law & Order**: High-contrast typography and a 1:1 color-matched palette across themes.
